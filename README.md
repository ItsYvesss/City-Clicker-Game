# [City Clicker Game](https://itsyvesss.github.io/City-Clicker-Game/)


City Clicker Game – Features:

🏙️ Build Your City: Start small and expand into a huge metropolis.

💰 Click to Earn Coins: Tap to collect coins and grow your city.

⚡ 50+ Upgrades: Boost coins per click, passive income, and unlock new perks.

✨ Passive Income: Earn coins automatically over time.

🎨 Animations & Particle Effects: Enjoy a lively and engaging city environment.

🏷️ City Naming System: Name your city, but respect the rules! Forbidden words are banned.

🚫 Strike & Ban System: Keep gameplay fair with warnings and penalties for rule-breaking.

🌐 Persistent Progress: Your city, upgrades, and stats are saved automatically.

🎯 Ultimate Goal: Become the most successful City Tycoon!
